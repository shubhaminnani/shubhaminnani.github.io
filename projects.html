Research Projects

Digital Pathology & Clinical AI

Vision Language Model for Pathology Report Generation (REG 2025)
Developed a vision-language model to automatically generate structured diagnostic reports from gigapixel whole-slide images. Submitted to the MICCAI 2025 REG challenge, achieving 5th place internationally.
WHO 2021 Glioma Classification from WSI
Multi-scale whole-slide image assessment pipeline for automated WHO 2021 glioma subtype classification using pathology foundation models and Multiple Instance Learning (MIL). Trained and validated on 2,500+ brain tumor cases from >25 clinical sites. Published in Neuro-Oncology. Won Best Paper at CCBB 2025.
IDH Mutation Biomarker Identification in Gliomas
Weakly supervised computational algorithm for IDH mutation status prediction from H&E-stained WSI using Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) based on Vision Transformers (ViT) and DINO. Eliminates dependency on expensive molecular testing. Published in Neuro-Oncology Advances.
Prognostic Risk Stratification of Gliomas
AI-based prognostic risk stratification in adult-type diffuse gliomas leveraging pathology foundation models and MIL architectures. Combines WSI morphology with survival prediction objectives for clinically actionable outputs.
Multimodal Prognostic Stratification of Glioblastoma (UPenn · CBICA)
Multimodal explainable AI model integrating histopathology imaging and clinical features for GBM patient prognostic stratification. Achieved AUC ≈ 0.75 on a multi-institutional test cohort of 188 GBM patients. Published in Modern Pathology and Frontiers in Neuroscience.
Oncocytic Renal Tumor Subtyping
Computational algorithm for AI-based subtyping of oncocytic renal tumors (Renal Oncocytoma, Low-Grade Oncocytic Tumor, Chromophobe RCC) from H&E-stained WSI. Published in Archives of Pathology & Laboratory Medicine.

Computer Vision & Prior Work

Skin Lesion Segmentation & Classification (ISIC Challenge)
GAN-based architecture for skin lesion segmentation combined with CNN-based classification using ResNets, MobileNets, Xception, and EfficientNets. Published in Nature Scientific Reports.
Retinal Fundus Glaucoma Analysis (REFUGE2 · MICCAI 2020)
Algorithm for optic disc/cup segmentation, glaucoma classification, and fovea localization from retinal fundus images using EfficientNets and UNet architectures. Ranked Top 10 internationally at MICCAI 2020.
Anomaly Pattern Segmentation in Agriculture (Agriculture-Vision · CVPRW 2020)
Feature Pyramid Network (FPN) encoder with varying decoder backbones for aerial agricultural image anomaly pattern recognition. Results published at CVPR 2021 Workshops.
Semantic Scene Segmentation · Indian Driving Dataset (AUTONUE)
Encoder-Decoder architecture (InceptionResNetV2, EfficientNets) for semantic scene segmentation in unstructured driving environments. Won 1st Prize at NCVPRIPG 2019 and 2nd Prize at ICVGIP 2020.
Automatic Number Plate Recognition (Nymo.ai)
End-to-end real-time ANPR system combining YOLO object detection and Mask-RCNN Region Proposal Networks with OCR integration. Delivered real-time inference at ~6-8 fps on edge devices.
Age-Related Macular Degeneration Detection (AMD Challenge · ISBI 2020)
CNN-based algorithms for AMD diagnosis and lesion segmentation from fundus photos. Ranked Top 10 internationally at IEEE ISBI 2020.
